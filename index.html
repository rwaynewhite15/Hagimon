✨ HAGIMON ✨

Invoke the Saints. Break the Powers of Sin.

🎯 The Goal

You are a pilgrim. The seven deadly sins each hold Power. Break every sin's power to zero before your three Resolve ❤ run out.

⚔️ Each Trial

Three sins manifest — choose your battle, then invoke a saint whose virtue counters it. Every option shows your true odds and how much power a victory would break. Win by a wide margin and the sin breaks faster.

📿 Your Resources

✠ Dulia unlocks new saints and pays each invocation — you begin with St. Jude alone. ☩ Grace raises your virtues in the Chapel, pleads for Providence after a lost trial, and can be given as alms (☩2 → ✠1) when your purse runs dry.

⚖️ The World Pushes Back

A loss feeds the sin — stronger and more often in your path. Spurned sins grow bolder. Sin festers as trials pass. A vanquished sin's malice flees into the survivors. And a saint who intercedes must rest. The war is a race: choose well, and finish it.

Every pilgrimage begins anew — only wisdom carries over.
